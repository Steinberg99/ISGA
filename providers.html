Mobile providers in the Netherlands

This is a list of all the mobile providers in the Netherlands. A mobile plan in the Netherlands consists of a certain amount of calling minutes and mobile data.

NPK is one of best mobile providers in the Netherlands, but beware it is one of the most expensive!

0800 0402 www.npk.com

2 Elet offers you the best bang for your buck, their mobile plans are the cheapest and offer the most data.

0800 1234 www.2elet.nl (dutch)

Oymis offers cheap plans with low amounts of data. If you are low on funds this is a great choice.

0800 5678 www.oysim.nl (dutch)

O Phone is a reliable but expensive provider. If you have the extra cash its a good choice.

0800 0402 www.OPhone.nl (dutch)
